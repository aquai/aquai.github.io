Research

My primary research interest lie within Natural Language Generation, particularly the theoretical aspects of the language generation, e.g., Referring Expression Generation, Quantified Expression Generation and so on. I also broadly interested in other language generation related issues, including Dialogue System, Surface Realisation, and Style Transfer.
